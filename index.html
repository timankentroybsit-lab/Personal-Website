Free Coding Modules

These are some free coding platforms that have helped me level up my programming skills. I highly recommend them for beginners and intermediate learners:

  • Codedex.io – A beginner-friendly platform with interactive lessons. Great for practicing JavaScript and Python.
  • FreeCodeCamp.org – Offers full courses and projects. I used it to build my first websites and improve my coding logic.
  • Codewars.com – Perfect for challenging your skills with coding “kata” problems. Helps improve problem-solving and algorithm skills.

💡 Tip: Try mixing these platforms depending on what you want to learn—Codedex for basics, FreeCodeCamp for projects, and Codewars for practice!
